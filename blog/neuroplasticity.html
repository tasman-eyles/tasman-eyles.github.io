Neuroplasticity and Growth Mindset

How understanding the principles of neuroplasticity benefits people

As discussed in the introduction video, the understanding of neuroplasticity can be used to favor recovery from injuries or illnesses, like strokes. If someone has an understanding of neuroplasticity, they can use it to their advantage by letting it encourage learning and skill development throughout their lives. The power of neuroplasticity also goes as far as to provide strategies to recover from mental health issues and strengthen resilience. It does this by highlighting the mind's ability to adapt and grow from experiences, and it can encourage people to make healthy decisions, like going to see a theorist, for example. Another interesting example of the benefits of understanding neuroplasticity would be its ability to enhance performance in physical and mental domains. For example, your ability to play instruments or to gain muscular strength, just by practicing in your mind.

What is a Growth Mindset and why is it relevant?

The growth mindset is where those with it believe that when facing obsticles, there is room for constant inprovement and development of their abilities. This opposes a fixed mindset, where people with this believe their intelligence, abilities, and talents are all just fixed traits, like having a cap
